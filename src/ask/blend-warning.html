What is blending?

You're about to blend into your current container.

Blending allows a domain that belongs to another container to also work in this container. This is useful when a site loads resources from a domain you've assigned elsewhere (e.g., a CDN or login provider).

⚠️ Use blending sparingly

  • Blending reduces container isolation by allowing domains to access multiple containers
  • This can potentially allow tracking across containers
  • Only blend domains you trust and that are necessary for functionality
